# Remote and Hybrid Work Policy

## Purpose and Scope
This policy defines the rules for remote and hybrid work arrangements at Nexora Technologies. It applies to all full-time employees whose role has been designated as remote-eligible by their department head. Roles requiring physical presence (e.g. facilities, on-site IT support, lab-based roles) are excluded and follow standard office attendance rules.

## Work Arrangement Categories
Nexora Technologies recognizes three work arrangement categories: Office-based (5 days per week on-site), Hybrid (minimum 3 days per week on-site, remaining days remote), and Fully Remote (approved case-by-case by department head and HR, typically for specialized roles or employees relocating for personal reasons). Default arrangement for all new hires is Hybrid unless the offer letter specifies otherwise.

## Hybrid Work Days
Hybrid employees must be present in the office on their team's designated anchor days, communicated by each manager at the start of each quarter. Outside of anchor days, employees may choose their remaining in-office days but must update their status in the HR portal calendar by 6 PM the prior working day so teams can plan collaborative work.

## Eligibility for Fully Remote Work
Employees may request fully remote status after completing 6 months of continuous employment and receiving a "Meets Expectations" or higher rating in their most recent performance review. Requests are submitted via the HR portal and require both manager and department head approval. Fully remote status is reviewed annually and may be revoked with 30 days' notice if business needs change.

## Equipment and Home Office Setup
Nexora Technologies provides a company laptop, monitor, and standard peripherals to all remote and hybrid employees. A one-time home office setup allowance of INR 15,000 (or local currency equivalent) is available to fully remote employees for desk, chair, or other ergonomic equipment, claimable through the expense portal within the first 60 days of remote approval. Employees are responsible for maintaining a stable internet connection; a monthly internet reimbursement of INR 1,500 is provided to fully remote employees.

## Working Hours and Availability
Remote and hybrid employees are expected to be reachable during core hours of 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM in their local time zone, regardless of arrangement. Outside core hours, employees may structure their schedule flexibly provided they complete their contracted hours and meet deliverables. Cross-time-zone teams should agree on overlap hours with their manager.

## Data Security for Remote Work
Employees working remotely must connect to company systems only via the Nexora VPN and must not use public, unsecured Wi-Fi networks to access confidential company data. Company laptops must not be used by family members or other non-employees. Physical documents containing confidential information must not be left unattended in shared or public spaces. Full data handling requirements are covered in the IT Acceptable Use Policy.

## International Remote Work
Working remotely from a country other than the employee's registered work location requires prior written approval from HR due to tax, legal, and payroll implications. Requests must be submitted at least 4 weeks in advance. Unapproved international remote work may result in payroll and compliance issues and is treated as a policy violation.

## Performance and Accountability
Remote and hybrid work arrangements do not change performance expectations. Managers evaluate output and deliverables rather than hours logged. Employees who are unreachable during core hours without prior notice, or who consistently miss deadlines while working remotely, may have their remote eligibility reviewed and, if necessary, reverted to office-based status.
