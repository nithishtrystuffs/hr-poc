# Leave Policy

## Purpose and Scope
This policy applies to all full-time employees of the Company across all office locations. It does not apply to interns or contractors, who are governed by their individual agreements. Leave year runs from January 1 to December 31 and does not carry over from a previous employer.

## Annual Leave
Full-time employees accrue 18 days of paid annual leave per calendar year, credited at 1.5 days per completed month of service. Annual leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 5 working days in advance for planned leave. Up to 5 unused annual leave days may be carried forward into the following year; any balance beyond that is forfeited on December 31. Carried-forward days must be used by March 31 of the new year or they lapse.

## Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave per calendar year. Sick leave does not carry forward to the next year. For absences of 3 or more consecutive working days, a medical certificate must be submitted to HR within 3 working days of returning to work. Sick leave cannot be used for pre-planned medical procedures unless supported by a doctor's note; those should be requested as annual leave or, where eligible, medical leave.

## Casual Leave
Employees receive 6 casual leave days per year for short-notice personal matters. Casual leave must be applied for at least 1 day in advance, except in genuine emergencies, where it may be regularized within 2 working days of return. No more than 2 consecutive casual leave days may be taken without manager approval; longer absences should be applied as annual leave.

## Maternity Leave
Employees who have completed at least 80 working days with the Company in the 12 months preceding the expected delivery date are eligible for 26 weeks of paid maternity leave. Up to 8 weeks may be availed before the expected delivery date, with the remainder taken after childbirth. Employees must notify HR and their manager at least 8 weeks before the intended start of leave, along with a medical certificate confirming the expected delivery date.

## Paternity Leave
Male employees are entitled to 15 calendar days of paid paternity leave, to be availed within 6 months of the child's birth or legal adoption. Paternity leave may be taken in a maximum of 2 blocks and requires 2 weeks' advance notice to the manager and HR, except where the birth occurs earlier than expected.

## Bereavement Leave
Employees may take up to 5 paid working days of bereavement leave in the event of the death of an immediate family member (spouse, child, parent, or sibling), and up to 3 paid working days for the death of a grandparent, parent-in-law, or grandchild. This leave does not need to be applied for in advance and can be regularized with HR upon return.

## Unpaid Leave
Employees who have exhausted all applicable paid leave categories may request unpaid leave of up to 30 calendar days per year, subject to manager and HR approval. Requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance except in emergencies. Unpaid leave exceeding 15 consecutive days may affect the accrual of annual leave and benefits eligibility for that period; employees should confirm impact with HR before applying.

## Public Holidays
The Company observes 10 paid public holidays per year, as published in the HR portal calendar at the start of each calendar year. Employees required to work on a public holiday due to business need are entitled to a compensatory day off within the following 30 days, to be scheduled with manager approval.

## Leave Approval Process
All leave requests are submitted and tracked through the HR portal. Annual and casual leave require direct manager approval. Sick leave of 3 or more days, maternity leave, paternity leave, and unpaid leave additionally require HR sign-off. Employees can check their real-time leave balance at any time in the HR portal under "My Leave."
