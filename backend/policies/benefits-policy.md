# Employee Benefits Policy

## Purpose and Scope
This policy summarizes the benefits available to full-time employees of Nexora Technologies. Benefits eligibility begins on the employee's date of joining unless otherwise specified below. Interns and contractors are not eligible for benefits under this policy.

## Health Insurance
Nexora Technologies provides group health insurance coverage of INR 5,00,000 per employee per year, covering the employee, spouse, and up to 2 dependent children. Coverage begins from day 1 of employment. Employees may opt to add parents to the policy at a co-payment of 50% of the additional premium, deducted from monthly salary. Enrollment and dependent additions are managed through the HR portal within 30 days of joining or a qualifying life event (marriage, birth of a child).

## Life and Accident Insurance
All full-time employees are automatically covered under a group term life insurance policy equal to 3 times their annual base salary, at no cost to the employee. Accidental death and disability coverage of INR 10,00,000 is also provided. No enrollment is required; coverage begins automatically on the date of joining.

## Retirement Benefits
Nexora Technologies contributes 12% of an employee's basic salary to the Provident Fund each month, matched by an equal employee contribution as required by statute. Employees become eligible for gratuity after completing 5 years of continuous service, calculated as per the applicable statutory formula.

## Wellness Allowance
Employees receive an annual wellness allowance of INR 12,000, reimbursable for gym memberships, fitness classes, mental health counseling, or wellness apps. Claims are submitted through the expense portal with supporting receipts and must be used within the calendar year; unused amounts do not carry forward.

## Learning and Development
Each employee has an annual learning budget of INR 25,000 for courses, certifications, conferences, or books relevant to their role, subject to manager approval. Additionally, employees may apply for full sponsorship of one job-relevant certification exam per year through the "Certification Sponsorship" program administered by HR.

## Employee Assistance Program
All employees and their immediate family members have access to free, confidential counseling through the Employee Assistance Program, available 24/7 via phone or the partner app listed on the HR portal. This includes up to 6 counseling sessions per issue per year at no cost.

## Meal and Commute Benefits
Employees working from office locations receive a subsidized meal allowance of INR 150 per working day, credited to the company meal card. A commute reimbursement of up to INR 3,000 per month is available for employees using public transport or company-arranged cab services, claimable through the expense portal.

## Parental Benefits Beyond Leave
In addition to maternity and paternity leave under the Leave Policy, Nexora Technologies provides a one-time new-child allowance of INR 20,000 and reimburses up to INR 10,000 per year for daycare or creche expenses for children under age 5, for employees who have completed at least 1 year of service.

## Benefits Enrollment and Changes
Benefits elections made at the time of joining remain in effect for the year unless a qualifying life event occurs (marriage, birth/adoption of a child, change in marital status). Qualifying life events must be reported to HR within 30 days to update coverage; changes outside this window will only take effect at the next annual open enrollment period.
